April 2026 · Old Point planting

Native canopy
across Old Point
and Bottom House.

The work is straightforward: 1,800 native seedlings across four sites, planted by a crew of seven in the early hours before the heat. Old Point and Bottom House lost canopy faster than the rest of the island; the trees that hold become the canopy that, in ten years, Providencia walks under.
