## Style Prompt
AYRA transparency demo: dark public-wall canvas, field-document warmth, institutional operator clarity, and visible proof rails. The video should feel like a live system walkthrough for a public-good payment product: grounded, precise, privacy-aware, and specific to Providencia/Reforestation.

## Colors
- `#191714` public background, near the repo's `--public-bg`.
- `#f0eadc` public foreground, warm paper text.
- `#9adf82` verified leaf accent for approved/public states.
- `#c88a4b` earth accent for field work and receipt context.
- `#dfe5ef` operator surface for admin/steward panels.

## Typography
- Display: `Space Grotesk`, matching the live app display voice.
- Body: `IBM Plex Sans`, matching the live app body voice.
- Data: `Space Mono`, matching hashes, statuses, and amounts.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use generic neon SaaS blue/purple gradients.
- Do not expose private steward emails, raw receipt paths, or recipient names in public scenes.
- Do not present mock hashes as public proof; labels must say verified USDC or pending/internal where relevant.
- Do not center every scene as a floating title card; keep browser, queue, and receipt surfaces legible.
